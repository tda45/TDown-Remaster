## TDown Remaster

TDown Remaster is a Qt based front end to [yt-dlp](https://github.com/yt-dlp/yt-dlp), [gallery-dl](https://github.com/mikf/gallery-dl), [lux](https://github.com/iawia002/lux) and [svtplay-dl](https://svtplay-dl.se/). These are CLI tools and they deal with downloading of audio files, video files and image files from many websites.

### Features
- Download video and audio from multiple websites
- Batch downloading support
- Playlist downloading
- Multiple language support
- Cross-platform support (Windows, macOS, Linux)
